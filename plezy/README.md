A beautiful client for Plex & Jellyfin

Stream your media library with HDR, Dolby Vision, offline downloads, and a gorgeous experience across all your devices.

**Features**

- Wide Codec Support
- HDR10+
- Subtitles
- Cross-Platform
- Offline
- Watch Together
- Tracker Sync
- Skip intros
- Chapter support
- Gestures
